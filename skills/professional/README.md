# professional skills

Skills for managing people and shipping work inside an organization. Generic
across employers — nothing employer-specific lives here.

Examples of what goes here:
- People management workflows (1:1 prep, career coaching, scorecards)
- Performance review prep
- Hiring and interview workflows
- Cross-team stakeholder communication

If a skill is specific to your employer (internal tools, team names, private
processes), keep it in your private dotfiles instead.
